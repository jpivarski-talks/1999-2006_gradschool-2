Dilepton Widths of Y(1s), Y(2s) and Y(3s)
Jim Pivarski

Three points of comparison: dilepton widths of Y(1s), Y(2s) and Y(3s)

  • It will be a theoretical challenge to calculate and find agreement with each width individually.

  • To be a good test, these quantities will need correspondingly high precision experimental measurements. (They are currently measured to 3%, 5% and 7% precision, respectively.)

CESR/CLEO has dedicated 0.1 fb-1 to each resonance for the purpose of scanning the line shape, from which we will derive Gammaee.
