Dilepton Widths of Y(1s), Y(2s) and Y(3s)
Jim Pivarski

Before any data was taken, the effects of random jitter (high probability, small jump size) and random jumps (low probability, large size) were studied.

0.2 MeV - 0.3 MeV jitter has been deemed tolerable.

Beam energy calibration is actively being studied, and is read out and recorded every ten minutes for this purpose.
