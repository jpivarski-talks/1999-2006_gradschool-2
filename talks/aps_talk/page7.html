Dilepton Widths of Y(1s), Y(2s) and Y(3s)
Jim Pivarski

There is much work to be done!

  • Event selection cuts need to be defined (all data shown so far comes from pass1 calorimetry).

  • Efficiencies for those cuts need to be determined, both for counting hadrons and for measuring luminosity.

  • What energy systematics are actually present must be studied in greater detail, particularly the frequency of 0.5 MeV jumps.

Ultimately, we hope to provide a 2-3% measurement of Gammaee for each of the narrow upsilon resonances!
