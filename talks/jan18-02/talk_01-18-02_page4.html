Stability of the -2 MeV Point

With the possible exceptions of 121898, 122076 and 122556, the -2 MeV points are consistant with lying on the same Cross-section VS. Energy curve. The residuals of the points to the curve (January 10, 2001 Scan) are:

The sum of the square normalized residuals (i.e. chi2 ), excluding the two points from the January 10th scan itself, is 63.0074. The number of degrees of freedom is just the number of points (again, excluding runs 122993 and 123004), or 10, yielding a reduced chi^2 of 6.30.
